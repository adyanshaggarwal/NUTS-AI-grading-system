The Problem

Traditional grading methods are time-consuming and inconsistent. Educators spend 30+ hours per week on grading, leaving less time for teaching and personalized instruction.

This challenge is particularly acute in under-resourced settings, where teacher-to-student ratios are high. hence students miss out on personal guidance and attention.

As class sizes grow and educational resources remain constrained, this problem continues to worsen, creating an unsustainable burden on our education system. Teachers are thus overworked and cannot provide fair dedicated feedback to students.

Our Solution

Our AI-driven grading system revolutionizes assessment by evaluating both subjective and objective answers with LLM models featured in the Gemini API

The system provides detailed feedback on clarity and improvements. This ensures accurate, efficient grading with actionable insights, saving educators time by 80%

Our system adapts to different subjects, assignment types, and grading rubrics, making it versatile for various educational contexts.
